Pesto Pasta

Description

This pesto pasta recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredient

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Method

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
